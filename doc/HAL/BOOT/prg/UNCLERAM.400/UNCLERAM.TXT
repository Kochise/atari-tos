    RUNNING IT
    ----------
    UncleRam works as either an accessory or a program.

    FREE RAM
    --------
    This is the amount of the LARGEST block of free memory in your computer.
    This doesn't necessarily mean it is  ALL  the free memory you have left.

    TOS VERSION
    -----------
    This reports the version of TOS you have installed ( the version number
    of your operating system ).

    TOS DATE
    --------
    This shows the release date for your version of TOS.

    COLD
    ----
    Clicking on this button performs a  "Cold Reset"  of your computer. This
    is basically the same as turning your computer OFF and then back ON.

    WARM
    ----
    This is the same as pressing the "reset" button on the back of your
    computer.

    EXIT
    ----
    Click on this box to exit the alert box without any changes.
